# Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** January 2026

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs your use of the Parallax Intelligence Platform software.

## Permitted Uses

Parallax may be used for:

✅ **Authorized Security Testing** - Penetration testing with explicit written permission  
✅ **Bug Bounty Programs** - Participating in authorized bug bounty programs  
✅ **Security Research** - Academic and professional security research  
✅ **OSINT Investigations** - Open-source intelligence gathering from public sources  
✅ **Defensive Security** - Identifying vulnerabilities in systems you own or have authorization to test  
✅ **CTF Competitions** - Capture the Flag and similar authorized competitions  
✅ **Education & Training** - Learning and teaching cybersecurity concepts  
✅ **Compliance Auditing** - Internal security assessments and compliance checks  

## Prohibited Uses

You may NOT use Parallax for:

❌ **Unauthorized Access** - Accessing systems without explicit permission  
❌ **Privacy Violations** - Violating GDPR, CCPA, or other privacy laws  
❌ **Data Theft** - Stealing, copying, or exfiltrating data without authorization  
❌ **Harassment or Stalking** - Tracking individuals without consent  
❌ **Malware Distribution** - Creating or distributing malicious software  
❌ **Denial of Service** - Disrupting services or networks  
❌ **Social Engineering Attacks** - Unauthorized social engineering  
❌ **Bypassing Security Controls** - Circumventing security measures without permission  
❌ **Mass Scanning** - Indiscriminate scanning of networks without authorization  
❌ **Illegal Surveillance** - Unauthorized monitoring or surveillance  
❌ **Export Control Violations** - Violating export control regulations  
❌ **Any Illegal Activities** - Activities that violate applicable laws  

## Authorization Requirements

Before using Parallax for security testing, you MUST have:

1. **Written Permission** from the system owner
2. **Scope Definition** - Clear boundaries of what can be tested
3. **Time Window** - Defined testing period
4. **Contact Information** - Emergency contact if issues arise

## Legal Compliance

Users must comply with:

- Computer Fraud and Abuse Act (CFAA) - United States
- Computer Misuse Act - United Kingdom  
- Similar laws in your jurisdiction
- GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
- CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- All applicable local, state, and federal laws

## Enforcement

Violations of this AUP may result in:

- Immediate license termination
- Legal action including criminal prosecution
- Cooperation with law enforcement
- Civil liability for damages

## Responsible Disclosure

If you discover vulnerabilities while using Parallax:

1. Report them responsibly to the affected organization
2. Allow reasonable time for remediation (typically 90 days)
3. Do not publicly disclose details until patched
4. Follow coordinated disclosure practices

## Reporting Violations

To report violations of this AUP:

- Email: security@parallax.app
- Subject: "AUP Violation Report"
- Include: Details, evidence, affected parties

## Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this AUP at any time. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the modified AUP.
